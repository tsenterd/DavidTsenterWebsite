About Me

We design & develop qaulity products to make people's life better and easier. What's the use of technology if it can't make life easier?
Developing And Loving Every Minute

My name is David, and I fell in love with coding ever since I took my first programming course in grade 9. Since then, I have completed the AP Computer science A course, and have been succesful in several programming competitions such as recieving 18th place in the Educational Computing Organization of Ontario (ECOO) programming contest.

 

I also enjoy working on programming and technology-related projects outside of class. The majority of my spare time is spent learning new languages and algorithms. Some of my side projects include 'Speakeasy', a voice recognition based game designed to teach simple English phrases, as well as an IR robot I created that can solve any maze it is put in.

 

My work experience consists mainly of IT work. I spent two summers interning in the IT department at Kobo inc, a Canadian based e-reader development company. I gained tremendous knowledge relevant to the field, and am thankful to have had the opportunity to be a part of the team. In my time at Kobo, I developed my first formal powershell script, which automates the creation of users in the active directory database for the domain. It can be found in the portfolio section of this website.
